ReFeel Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 12, 2026

Last Updated: March 12, 2026

1. Introduction

ReFeel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy.

ReFeel is designed as a private emotional insight platform. Your emotional data is deeply personal, and our architecture is designed to respect and protect that privacy.

This Privacy Policy explains:

  • What data we collect
  • How we use it
  • How it is stored
  • When (if ever) it is shared
  • Your rights and controls

By using ReFeel, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

2. Core Privacy Philosophy

ReFeel is built on the principle that your emotional data belongs to you.

  • Your emotional moments remain private
  • Insights are generated primarily on-device
  • Your reflections are never sold
  • Your personal entries are never made public

3. Information We Collect

Account Information

When creating an account we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Username
  • Authentication credentials

This information is used only for account creation, authentication, and recovery.

Emotional and Reflection Data

You may enter:

  • Emotions
  • Contextual reflections
  • Tags or keywords
  • Structured reflections
  • Time capsule entries

This data allows ReFeel to generate insights and detect recurring emotional patterns.

Most analysis is performed directly on your device.

4. Usage Data

We may collect limited technical data such as:

  • App opens
  • Feature interactions
  • Crash reports
  • Performance logs

This data helps us improve the reliability and performance of the application.

5. Data Storage

ReFeel prioritizes on-device processing to minimize exposure of sensitive personal data.

Some account-related data may be stored securely on encrypted servers.

All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.

6. Payment Processing

If paid features become available, payments may be processed through trusted payment providers such as Paystack.

ReFeel does not store full payment card details. Payment information is handled securely by the payment provider.

7. Data Sharing

We do not share personal emotional entries with:

  • Advertisers
  • Third-party marketers
  • Public platforms

Data may only be shared if:

  • Required by law
  • Necessary for secure infrastructure services
  • You explicitly consent

8. Data Retention

We retain your data while your account remains active or as required by law.

You may request deletion of your account at any time.

9. Your Rights

You may have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Request data export
